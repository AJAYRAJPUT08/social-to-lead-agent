Product Name: LeadFlow AI

Description:
LeadFlow AI is a SaaS platform that helps businesses automate social media conversations and convert them into qualified leads using AI-powered chat agents.

Key Features:
- Automated replies for Instagram and LinkedIn DMs
- AI-based intent detection to identify high-intent users
- Lead capture and qualification
- Integration-ready for CRM systems

Pricing:
Starter Plan: ₹999/month
Pro Plan: ₹2999/month

Target Users:
Small and medium businesses, agencies, and startups looking to automate lead generation from social platforms.
